    Activity 1:

    Reading the pattern piece

    Description of activity:

    Students learn how to read the pattern piece envelop by answering a range of questions which they may choose to utilise for their folio documentation.

    Instructions:

    Referring to the pattern envelope for the garment item you want to make, fill in the worksheet attached below. Submit the completed file in the appropriate google classroom tile.

    Task analysis:

    This task is designed to help students become familiarised with reading and using commercial pattern pieces. This task addresses outcomes P2.2 (develops competence in the selection and use of appropriate manufacturing techniques and equipment), P2.3 (manages the design and manufacture of textile projects) and P3.2 (justifies the selection of fabrics, yarns and fibres for end-uses) within the preliminary Textiles and Design curriculum (NESA, 2013b). By encouraging students to understand and apply their knowledge (Armstrong, 2010) as they work to complete the worksheet they are provided, students are actively comprehending new text types (Australian curriculum, n.d.a) and using decimals and measurements (Australian curriculum, n.d.b) which support both literacy and numeracy general capabilities. Their learning is supported with the appropriate use of resources, custom made to assist students with this activity specifically in addition to as well as using various modes of communication (visual and written) in order to meet a range of student learning needs (AITSL, n.d.).
